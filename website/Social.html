Social Events

Bar One is the Students’ Union bar and is a great place to socialise throughout the week. The bar has a wide range of alcoholic and soft drinks as well as great food. Bar One has a wifi, comfy sofas and everything else you need.

Every Thursday is karaoke night which is free entry from 9pm till 1:30am. There is also a quiz night every Sunday. Every week there is a different theme. Again, this is free entry from 9pm till 11pm.

Foundry is music and club night venue at the Students’ Union. It has 3 rooms and multiple bars. Popular events such as Pop Tarts and Roar are some of the best student nights out in a safe and friendly environment.
