Our Accessability Statement

We are fully committed to making the webpages accessible to all.

Our website aims to follow the guidelines set out by The Worldwide Web Consortium for making web content accessible for users with disabilities.

Should you have difficulty in accessing the content of this site, please detail the problem in a contact form on the Contact Us Page.

Click here to see the guidelines for yourself.

Some features that we have included:

  • Contrasting colours for the visually impaired
  • Alt tags to images for aid screen readers
  • Links are highlighted when you hover over them
  • The website will work on a wide range of browsers to support those using alternative browsers

Privacy statement

We are fully committed to making the website a secure and trustworthy place to hand over personal information.

To do this the website follows GDPR.
